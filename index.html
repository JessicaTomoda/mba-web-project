Introdution

Welcome to Travel Advice! Here you are going to find many things to help with your trip.

Before we start take a look in the list below about the fist step to get ready for your trip.

  1. Make sure to chek you passport experation date.
  2. Use a suitcase tag to identify your luggage.
  3. Do not waste time, do research about the place you are traveling before getting there.

When deciding to travel anywhere, try to know a little about the local culture, famous places and most visited tourist spots. Look for tickets in advance, so you can save on your flight. Many hotels offer free transfers to many tourist spots, getting informed is a good option.
